# Privacy Policy

**InsightAlpha AI and LYRA**  
**Last updated:** February 14, 2026

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the InsightAlpha AI website, dashboard, and LYRA features on free and paid plans.

By using the platform, you acknowledge the data practices described in this policy.

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data required to operate, secure, and improve the service.

- Account information (such as name, email, profile metadata)
- Authentication identifiers from sign-in providers
- Subscription and plan metadata
- Product usage and interaction logs
- Device and technical telemetry (for reliability and abuse prevention)

If you provide portfolio or watchlist information, that data is processed to deliver analytics requested by you.

## 3. Data We Do Not Intentionally Collect

We do not intentionally collect biometric identifiers, government identity numbers, or sensitive personal data unless explicitly required by law and validly consented.

We do not request brokerage passwords or bank credentials inside the platform.

## 4. How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Provide dashboards, analytics, and AI-assisted explanations
- Enforce plan limits and prevent misuse
- Monitor reliability and improve platform quality
- Communicate important account and service updates

We do **not** sell personal data.

## 5. AI and Model Operations

LYRA may use deterministic analytics outputs and licensed market context to generate responses.

Prompt and response logs may be retained for:

- Safety and abuse monitoring
- Reliability and quality improvements
- Operational auditability

Your personal portfolio data is not used to train foundation AI models without explicit consent.

## 6. Sharing with Service Providers

We share data only with trusted processors that are necessary for platform delivery, including authentication, billing, hosting, and monitoring providers.

These providers are contractually restricted to approved processing purposes.

## 7. Security and Retention

We apply encryption in transit, role-based access controls, and operational safeguards appropriate to data sensitivity.

Data is retained only as long as required for service delivery, legal compliance, and legitimate dispute resolution.

## 8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law (including India’s DPDP Act, where applicable), you may request:

- Access to your personal data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Deletion of eligible data
- Restriction or withdrawal of consent for optional processing

Requests can be sent to **support@fusionwaveai.com**.

## 9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for session continuity, performance, and security diagnostics.

Where required by law, consent controls are provided.

## 10. Children

The platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.

If you believe a minor has submitted personal data, contact us for review and removal.

## 11. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy periodically.

Material changes will be communicated through the platform or associated channels.

## 12. Contact and Governing Law

For privacy or grievance requests, contact **support@fusionwaveai.com**.

This policy is governed by applicable laws of India, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.
