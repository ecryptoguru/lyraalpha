## Executive Summary
LyraAlpha AI is a financial-intelligence platform built around a durable architectural rule:

**the engines compute, the AI interprets.**

That principle remains the foundation of the product today, and it also defines the next chapter of the platform.

In its current implementation, LyraAlpha AI combines deterministic scoring, regime-aware framing, plan-aware AI routing on GPT-5.4 (nano / mini / full), and a dual-agent product surface across equities, ETFs, mutual funds, crypto, and commodities. The next phase evolves the product into a more reactive, persistent, and premium intelligence system — with **Convex** as the long-term primary app-state backend and deeper premium workflow design as the primary value lever.

This document is intentionally written in two layers:
- **implementation-aligned truth** about the current platform
- **future-state direction** for the next stage of product maturity

---

## Core Product Thesis
Retail investors do not mainly suffer from a shortage of information. They suffer from a shortage of interpretive structure.

LyraAlpha AI addresses that gap through:
- deterministic score engines
- market-regime framing
- compatibility and signal layers
- Lyra for market analysis
- Myra for support, onboarding, and monetization guidance

The product is not best described as a generic finance chatbot. It is better understood as a structured interpretation layer on top of computed market context.

---

## The Current Product Foundation
### 1. Deterministic Intelligence Layer
The platform computes six core score dimensions:
- Trend
- Momentum
- Volatility
- Liquidity
- Trust
- Sentiment

These scores form the base analytical language of the product and are complemented by regime state, compatibility framing, and asset-specific overlays.

### 2. Context and Workflow Layer
The product adds:
- discovery and comparison context
- premium workflow surfaces
- historical and cross-market framing
- plan-aware depth controls
- region-aware interpretation across US and India

### 3. AI Layer
The AI layer is intentionally separated by responsibility:
- **Lyra** interprets market context and analytical signals
- **Myra** handles support, credits, billing/help flows, and product guidance

This separation remains a strategic advantage because it improves governance, reliability, and user clarity.

## The Next Chapter
The next stage of LyraAlpha AI is not a departure from the current architecture. It is an expansion of it.

### Future-State Thesis
The platform should evolve into:
- a more reactive intelligence product
- a more persistent user-context system
- a more workflow-driven premium experience
- a more enterprise-ready collaborative platform

Three ideas define that transition:

### 1. GPT-5.4 — Now Live Across All Plans
GPT-5.4 (nano / mini / full) is the live analytical engine for all Lyra paths via Azure OpenAI Responses API. The routing model is complete and simplified to single-mode across all plans:
- STARTER: nano (SIMPLE/MODERATE), mini (COMPLEX)
- PRO: mini (SIMPLE/MODERATE), full · single (COMPLEX)
- ELITE/ENTERPRISE: mini (SIMPLE/MODERATE), full · single (COMPLEX)

The objective going forward is not a model upgrade — it is deepening workflow quality, prompt contract discipline, and premium experience polish on top of the existing model infrastructure.

### 2. Convex as the Long-Term Primary App-State Backend
The current runtime remains built on Next.js, Prisma/Postgres, and Redis/Upstash. That remains the truth today.

The future direction is to position **Convex** as the long-term primary backend for application state and reactive product surfaces.

That matters because the product increasingly benefits from:
- live session continuity
- reactive support flows
- faster feed freshness
- richer agent state and saved intelligence
- collaborative and team-oriented product behavior

The value is not backend novelty. The value is a more responsive product with less glue code and a stronger real-time user experience.

### 3. Premium Workflow Maturity
The moat of LyraAlpha AI is not a single model name. It comes from the combination of:
- deterministic computation
- constrained interpretation
- disciplined routing
- credits-first monetization
- premium workflow design

The product should continue to make Elite and Enterprise feel better primarily through workflow quality, context depth, and user experience rather than by simply generating longer answers.

## User Value by Tier

- entry-level discovery and education
- lighter analytical depth
- current runtime default of 50 monthly credits

### Pro
- daily-use analytical tier
- materially larger usage envelope
- stronger continuity for routine users

### Elite

- deepest flagship analytical experiences

- sales-assisted positioning
- future expansion path for persistent team intelligence, collaboration, and administrative visibility
