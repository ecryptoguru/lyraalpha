## Key Concept

The narratives page helps you see **which market stories are actually gaining force**. Instead of looking at isolated assets, it frames what themes, sectors, and macro ideas are attracting attention and which ones are losing sponsorship.

It is a context layer for understanding what the market seems to care about right now.

## What It Means

- Narratives help explain *why* multiple assets may be moving together.
- A strong narrative can support an asset setup, but it can also become crowded.
- The best use of narratives is to connect **theme strength** with **asset-level evidence**.
- If a narrative is weakening while an asset still looks strong, that tension matters.

## What It Does NOT Mean

- A popular narrative is **not** automatically a good trade.
- Narratives are **not** substitutes for asset-level analysis.
- A weak narrative does **not** mean every related asset will fail.
