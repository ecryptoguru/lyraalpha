## Key Concept

Rewards are designed to reinforce **good product habits**: learning, exploration, and disciplined interaction with the platform.

XP, streaks, badges, and bonus path rewards turn consistent usage into visible progress.

## What It Means

- Completing modules builds XP and helps level progression.
- Streaks reward consistency, not one-off bursts.
- Badges highlight mastery across specific learning themes.
- Curated paths can unlock bonus XP when you complete the full sequence.

## What It Does NOT Mean

- Rewards are **not** investment performance guarantees.
- More XP does **not** mean every analysis decision is correct.
- The goal is **not** to grind points without understanding the product.

## Quick Check

- [x] XP and badges are tied to meaningful product actions like learning progress. | Correct — the system is meant to reinforce useful behavior.
- [ ] A higher XP total means your market calls will automatically be better. | No — rewards track participation and literacy, not guaranteed outcomes.
- [x] Curated paths can include extra bonus rewards on top of module completion. | Yes — path completion can unlock bonus XP.
- [ ] Streaks are mainly about doing as much as possible in a single day. | No — streaks reward consistency over time.
