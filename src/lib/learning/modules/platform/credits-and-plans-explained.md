## Credits & Plans Explained

Credits are the operational currency for Lyra queries. Every plan includes a monthly allocation that resets automatically.

### Monthly Credit Allocations

| Plan | Monthly Credits |
|---|---|
| STARTER | 50 |
| PRO | 500 |
| ELITE | 1,500 |
| ENTERPRISE | 1,500 |

### How Credits Are Spent

| Query Type | Cost |
|---|---|
| SIMPLE query | 1 credit |
| MODERATE query | 3 credits |
| COMPLEX query | 5 credits |
| Compare Assets | 5 + 3 per additional asset |
| Shock Simulator | 5 + 3 per additional asset |

### Monthly Reset

Your credits reset to your plan's base amount at the start of each billing cycle. Unused credits do not carry over — use them or lose them.

### What Plans Unlock Beyond Credits

Credits alone don't tell the full story. Plans also unlock:

- **Model quality** — STARTER gets nano/mini; PRO/ELITE/ENTERPRISE get full model on COMPLEX queries
- **Premium workflows** — Compare Assets and Shock Simulator require ELITE or ENTERPRISE
- **Context depth** — higher plans get more RAG context, memory, and cross-sector signals
- **Web research** — live research augmentation is gated to higher tiers

### Referral Bonus

Refer a friend and you earn 75 credits when they activate (use 10 credits). They get 50 credits on sign-up.

## Quick Check

- [x] STARTER and ELITE users pay the same credit cost for a COMPLEX query. | Correct — credit costs are uniform across plans. What differs is the model quality and features unlocked.
- [ ] Unused monthly credits roll over to the next month. | False — credits reset to the plan's base amount each cycle.
- [ ] Referrals give the referrer 50 credits. | No — the referrer gets 75 credits after the referred user activates. The referred user gets 50 on sign-up.
- [x] Compare Assets costs more credits than a standard COMPLEX query. | Correct — it uses multi-asset pricing: 5 + 3 per additional asset.
