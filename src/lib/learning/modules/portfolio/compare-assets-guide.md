## Key Concept

When deciding between two assets (e.g., AAPL vs MSFT, or BTC vs ETH), looking at price charts is insufficient. **Comparative Asset Analysis** places assets side-by-side and evaluates them across every engine in the LyraAlpha AI ecosystem simultaneously.

It reveals the *structural differences* driving performance.

## The Compare Matrix

The Compare tool strips away the noise and provides a direct, head-to-head breakdown:

1. **Signal Strength Head-to-Head**: Which asset has higher systemic conviction right now?
2. **Engine Score Comparison**: A granular look at the sub-scores. Asset A might have a higher overall Signal Strength, but Asset B might have significantly stronger Momentum and Liquidity. You can see exactly where the divergence lies.
3. **Performance Returns**: Side-by-side return data across 1W, 1M, 3M, YTD, and 1Y periods. 
4. **Factor Alignment (ARCS)**: Which asset is a better fit for the *current* market regime? This is often the deciding factor between two otherwise strong assets.

## Finding the Edge

The goal of comparison is to find the **structural edge**. 

For example, if you compare two semiconductor stocks, and both have high Trend scores, but Stock A has a Sentiment score of 85 (heavy accumulation) while Stock B has a Sentiment score of 40 (distribution), you have found the edge. Stock A is structurally superior, even if their charts look identical today.

## Lyra Synthesis

The Compare tool includes a dedicated Lyra interface. You can ask Lyra direct, comparative questions:
- "Which of these has better risk/reward right now?"
- "Which is more aligned with a Defensive regime?"
- "Why does Asset A have a higher score despite lower recent returns?"

Lyra synthesizes all the engine data to provide a plain-English verdict.
